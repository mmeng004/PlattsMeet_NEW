How PlattsMeet Began

PlattsMeet started as a Software Design Studio project by four SUNY Plattsburgh students. We were two international students and two transfer students, so we understood the difficulties of meeting new people at a new school. Developing under the constraints of the Covid-19 pandemic, we realized that a central social media hub tied directly to SUNY Plattsburgh was needed more than ever. When all classes were online and all club activities put on hold or held over zoom, it was especially difficult to meet classmates, make new friends, and build lasting connections.

Our Mission

We want students at SUNY Plattsburgh to have an easy way to meet, talk, and connect. Finding like-minded people, making friends, discovering new activities, joining clubs, and creating study groups are all a big part of college life. PlattsMeet aims to facilitate these endeavors by centralizing services performed by many other social media sites into one safe and easily accessible site built only for students!
